Samurai

Plasma Beam

1000 Samurai Sashes, 100 Plasma Cores, 1000 Zorlian Hearts, Ragnarok, Zorlian Notebook, Leviathan Scale, Zorlyte required. First go to Shinjoku City and go inside the Samurai House, below that map you will need to collect 1000 Samurai Sashes. Then you will need to go trade it in to Tharsin, he's located somewhere in Shinjoku. After that you will receive an Ancient Katana, so head to top right of the city and open the statue with it. When you get inside you will need to collect 100 Plasma Cores by killing the Plasma Generator(s) (1/25) which can be located in the bottom right of the Icefield and it is a blue zone meaning mages can't cast there. When you are done you will need to collect 1000 Zorlian Hearts which is drop by the Zorlian (Note: The blue ones do not drop the hearts.) When you are finished with that you will need to get a 'Zorlian Notebook' droped by the Zorlian General. After that you will need to find a hidden passage in the Icefield to kill Leviathan which drops his scale (1/25). Then head to the top of the Icefield and trade it in for a 'Undead Key' then go back to the cave the first map of the city and open the passage to get to 'Undead King Froyst' (Caution: He deals a lot of damage, so bring a group of friends to team him.) Next, you will need a Ragnarok droped from him at a rate of 1/20. It also drops the Samurai Class Buff which is 1/20 as well. After you are done with that you will need to run into the Icefield Temple which is located in the top right of the Icefield. When you get to the end you will need to fight 'King Zorlian' he drops an item called 'Zorlyte' (1/75). After you are done with that you need to go back to Tharstin City and find the shop and trade it in. Now you have completed the Plasma Beam Quest, Congrats.

Dragoon

Soul Scythe

1500 Dragon Bone(s), 4 Quarter Key(s), 1 Tanem's Soul, 1 Hayashi's Soul, 1 Hatil's Soul, 1 Serra's Soul, 80 Dragoon's Essence(s), 1000 Mysterious Eye(s), 1 Cyrillius' Lance, 1 Lethailus' Pendant required. First head to Ryoko and find your way to the Dragon's Den. Now, collect 1500 Dragon Bone(s) either in the den or keep going north until you reach the trail and collect there. Kill Winged Dragon's or Cave Bat's. Once you have the Dragon Bones, You can trade them in for an Ivory Rod at Ryoko Camp inside the tent. (North/East ish when you've hit the Ryoko Trail.) Next head North/West and be prepared for the run threw the Ryoko Trail, to Ryoko Ruins then finally to Synical's Camp. Once you arrive at Synical's Camp you can either kill the Gate Keepers for a key drop, or run around the outskirts of the camp looking for a secret entrance inside. Once inside you need to kill the Lieutenant General(s) until you get a Quarter Key drop, then use that key on the cabins with different coloured flags outside of them, to get to the bosses. Then kill each boss, for their soul drops. Tanem's, Hayashi's, Hatil's, and Serra's. Reminder: You need FOUR Quarter Keys to trade for further items so you might as well get those before proceeding. Now head back to where you traded the Dragon Bone(s) for the Ivory Rod and go left one map, inside the other tent and trade in all the needed items. If you traded them all in, you should now have an Entrance Key, now go back inside Synical's Camp and head North to the big tent, use the Entrance Key there and collect 80 Dragoon's Essence(s) from either the Lance Specialists or the Fallen Dragoons (Fallen Dragoons have a better rate, but they're both really crappy.) Once you have all those, head North until you reach the Janitor, now trade your Essence(s) for the Throne Opener, take your Throne Opener and use it on the blue door ahead of you. Inside is a boss named Lethailus you must kill him until he drops his Pendant, once you have that use the Pendant on the black tiles behind the thrones. Now you're in Aquias, the next three maps are tricky because there are various warps spread on the map, it'll take some time to figure out how the warps work. Once you're at the final map and you see two waterfalls, you can use your Pendant on the Water in the middle of the map, and head up into Cyrillus' Lair. Kill Cyrillus until he drops his Lance. Here you must hit the switches to get further. First go to the right pillar at the top of the map, use the Pendant there and walk onto the tile, now head over to the other pillar and go up then back down you will see a few helmets on the wall, enter the ones on the right side, left into the wall and up. Here's the Janitor again, now you must kill the Mysterious Hoods until you have 1000 Mysterious Eye(s) then trade them with the Janitor for the Synical Key. (Fyi; This hall is pretty tricky, it's a maze to get to the end;; you must go right up, right up.) Now use the Synical Key on the blue door and head into Synical's Lair kill Synical for the Final Soul. You can finally leave this place... run all the way back to Ryoko go to the map with the Grand Wyrm and you will see an entrance to a cliff, enter and trade all your items into Grazian, walk up to the wall on the right and use your Final Soul key on the wall, into another map with Grazian, trade the final soul and handle for the Soul Scythe. Congratz!

Rogue

Dragon Claws

900 Drumsticks, 1500 Dragonian Souls, 3500 Cantar Bones, 500 Starlit Ores, Ka Ru Claws, 250 Bomb Shelling required. To start Dragon Claws you first need to go to Kenshi which is located near the bottom left of the World Map. When you arrive at Kenshi you need to collect 900 Drumsticks from the Roruni Falcon which drop Drumsticks (3 per kill). After you have collected 900 you will need to find the stop inside the city of Kenshi and trade it for a Gate Key. After you have the use it to get to Kenshi Plains which is East of the city. You will then need to locate a big cave in the plains that is called 'Ka Ru'. Once you have entered you will need to collect 1500 Dragonian Soul(s) which is dropped by Bleak Spirit that drop 3 (rate 1/3). After you have collected it you will need to collect 3000 Cantar Bones which can be dropped by several npcs (Cantar Overlord, Cantar Berserker, Cantar Warrior). I would highly suggest you kill Cantar Overlord for the bones because it drop 6 (1/2) rate. After you are done, head to the Starlit Mines and collect 500 Starlit Ores (1/2 for 1). After you are done you will need to go back to Lair of Ka Ru and collect another 500 extra Cantar Bones. Then head to the city of Sel Crue, find the shop and trade 500 Cantar Bones and 250 Bomb Shelling (Which can be bought in the shop for 10k per) to get a 'Large Bomb.' Once you have done that head back to the lair and run to the end of the first part, there you will find a gate that will require a key (Large Bomb.) But beware during the run there is a map with 200 damage tiles so you will need to unequip and run accross to not get damage at all. Beyond the door of the first part, you will need to run the lair which is very difficult so I highly suggest you get a group to do the run. Once you get to the end you will need to fight the boss "Ka Ru" (Be very aware of the flame spirit, it hits HARD.) Ka Ru drops an item called 'Ka Ru Claws,' you will need to get two of them (1/40 rate). After you have succeeded getting the 2 drops you will need to head to Lu Thaye which is the island located just below Kenshi. From there you will need to run to the end of the island to find the shop and trade it in for your Dragon Claws. Congratulations you have completed the quest.
