Aelfric's Stiletto

200 Terror Eyes, 250 Barbs, Anchor, 1250 Tainted Blood, 1000 Tainted Fangs, Counter Seal, Aelfric's Wing, Bloody Rose, Blood Siphon required. First you will need to collect an Anchor to enter the Isle of Sel Crue. On the East of the World Map, you'll find Terror of the Deeps and Sea Urchins. These drops the Terror Eyes and Barbs. When you have the full ammount, go to the bottom left of Alkanor, enter the shop and trade for an Anchor. Now make your way to Sel Crue by going East of the World Map. Find the little island called Mesa, and travel through until you come to the town. Go to the top right hand map, and use your Anchor on the boat (Doesn't dissolve). Head North a couple maps into the islands forest area. Here you'll encounter Daywalkers if it's day, and Bloodlusts if it's night. There's plenty of maps to collect on, so take your pick. When you have both the Fangs and Blood, go to the bottom right hand house in the town and enter. Trade the Fangs and Blood for a Counter Seal, and use it on the door above you (Doesn't dissolve). Here you'll find Aelfric [Bat] who drops the Wing you need. Go back out into the storehouse and trade for the Bloody Rose. Now travel to the top left map of the island, and walk into the cavern behind the wooden panels. Go up 2 maps until there's a door, and use the Bloody Rose on the door (Doesn't dissolve). In this room is the final boss, Aelfric Keat, who drops the Blood Siphon (Rare drop). Once you've got the Blood Siphon, trade your Bloody Rose and Blood Siphon in Keats room, for Aelfric's Stiletto.
