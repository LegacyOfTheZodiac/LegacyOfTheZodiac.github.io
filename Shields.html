Aegis Shield

Goregof Hide, 1,200 Shield Fragments, 300 Mauler Pelts required. An NPC called Goregof drops a Goregof Pelt, who is located inside a cave on the way to Sub Zero town. You then need to go and collect 1200 Shield Fragments, which are dropped by Alkanor Spear-Knights, located at Alkanor, near the yellow gate inside the house. Then you need 450 Mauler Pelts, which are dropped by Maulers, located on the Hidden Island. There is also one Mauler in a white zone, on the way to Kenshi town, there is a small cave where you can kill the Mauler safely. Once you have the materials, go inside one of the Western houses in Alkanor for the final trade in. Congratulations.
